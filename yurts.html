The Yurts at Pacicfic Trails

What is a yurt

Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.

How are the yurts furnished

Each yurts is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stoves. The luxury camping experience also includes elecricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge

What should i bring

Bring a sense of adventure and some time to relax! Most guests also pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with layers of clothing.
